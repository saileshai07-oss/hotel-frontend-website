Our Philosophy

Where Coastal Serenity Meets Heritage Luxury

Founded with a vision to redefine the modern getaway, Hotel Grand Horizon serves as a quiet sanctuary along the pristine coastline. We balance sophisticated architectural design with natural landscapes to create an unforgettable retreat.

Key Facts About Grand Horizon:

  • Located exclusively on 742 Ocean Edge Drive, Cliffside Haven.
  • Architectural footprint configured to match 100% renewable coastal air-flow designs.
  • Home to 20 signature eco-luxury master amenities and custom suites.

Every detail, from the layout of our open-air panoramic lounges to the locally sourced seasonal ingredients in our coastal kitchens, is mindfully selected to help you unwind and reconnect.
